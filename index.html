About Me

My name is Karan Pal, and I am working as a project assistant under Assistant Prof. Georgia Panopoulou at Chalmers, where my project aims to measure the 3D properties of the magnetic field in the Galactic ISM by reducing stellar polarization data from an optical polarimeter and to combine those measurements with data from Gaia and Planck.
